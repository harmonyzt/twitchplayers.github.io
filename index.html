MORE THAN JUST A SAIN PRESET

Twitch Players is more than just a mod - it's a new way to experience SPT, making your experience with fights as close as possible to Live Escape From Tarkov. Not only does it allow you to customize bot personalities in its own way aside from SAIN but it also adds Twitch Streamers into the game, making every raid even more unique and unpredictable. On top of that, it comes with a SAIN Preset featuring Progressive Difficulty Scaling: as your PMC levels up, AI enemies become more challenging and more accurate. This ensures the game stays exciting and demanding, even as you grow into a seasoned player.

FEATURES

  • Customizable SAIN Personalities: Have your own way with personalities SAIN features to match your preferred level of challenge.
  • Your Own Names Integration: Add your friends as enemies and give them that Rat personality.
  • Progressive SAIN Difficulty Scaling: The difficulty of AI scales with your PMC level, ensuring a challenging and progressive experience.
  • Dynamic Personalities: AI can have dynamically generated personalities each server start up, let it be all of them, or just Twitch Players.
  • Compatibility with Fika: Compatible* with Fika. (Make sure you have ModSync installed!)
  • Easy Installation and Configuring: Simple installation process, with automatic SAIN preset installation and updates throughout mod releases.

PLAY SOLO OR WITH FRIENDS

Whether you prefer to go solo or team up with friends, Twitch Players mod enhances your experience in both scenarios. The mod comes with customized SAIN preset and its behavior to suit your hardcore playstyle, making solo raids more intense and cooperative play more strategic. Every raid becomes will be unique, unpredictable and sometimes, disappointing.
